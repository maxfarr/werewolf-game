The big bad wolf wants to find love!

Dating is hard- especially when you're a werewolf!

You've finally scored dinner with your crush: remember to stay calm, make eye contact, engage in the conversation... and bring your lycanthropy antidote in case you transform.

All the Better to Date You With is a casual Warioware-like about hiding part of yourself, and the desire to finally step into the light.

Created in 2 weeks as part of Pirate Software - Game Jam 15

How to Play

Win condition: Make it through all five stages of your date without transforming

Challenge: Play match-3 to make sure your lycanthropy antidote doesn't overheat, and keep your date going through minigames.

Controls: Use your mouse to control all minigames.
